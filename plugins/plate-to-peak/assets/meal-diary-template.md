# Meal diary — Plate to Peak

The floor: **date, rough time, what you ate.** One line per meal. That's enough
to map meals to glucose spikes. More detail never hurts, but don't let detail
stop you logging — a two-word food name with a time beats a paragraph tomorrow.

## Format

2026-06-28
08:30  Huel shake
13:00  Chicken salad, bread
19:45  Salmon, rice, broccoli

2026-06-29
09:00  Porridge with berries
...

## Rules of thumb

- Time = when you STARTED eating, roughly. Within 15 minutes is plenty.
- Snacks count. A biscuit with coffee is a line.
- Drinks with sugar or milk count (juice, latte, kefir).
- Missed a day? Leave it out — don't reconstruct from memory a week later.
